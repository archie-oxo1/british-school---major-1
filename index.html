About The British School

The vision of the founders of The British School, ‘to prepare dynamic and caring citizens to meet the challenges of global society while retaining their traditional values’, has found its tangible reality in the provision of educational opportunities for a broad spectrum of learners - from the Primary to the Tertiary levels.

A harmonious integration of valued traditions with modern outlook is the guiding principle behind the development of the academic environment
